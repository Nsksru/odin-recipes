Gulab jamun is a beloved South Asian dessert consisting of soft, melt-in-the-mouth fried dumplings made from milk solids (khoya) or milk powder.


A mango lassi is a refreshing, traditional Indian beverage that blends sweet, ripe mangoes with yogurt, water or milk, and a touch of spice. Often described as a lighter, fruitier alternative to a milkshake, it is smooth, creamy, and delightfully cooling.


Cutlet Chaat is a popular Indian street food featuring crispy potato or vegetable cutlets topped with tangy chutneys, yogurt, and savory garnishes. It is easily made at home by frying cutlets until golden, then layering them with a flavorful combination of sauces and toppings
